Location

For group classes wee are located in Stonehall National School, which features fully
equipped classrooms, desks and interactive whiteboards. For 1-1 we can come to you.

Custom Notes

We provide hand crafted notes designed to condense the topics for the state exam into easy to use, readable and most importantly understandable chunks to use as a study aid come exam time.

The student will work through the examples in the notes in the lessons, which are broken up by topic with emphasis on identifying how to answer the question.

During your first session the student will be provided with a folder to use throughout the year. The notes for each section will be provided each week, to produce a study guide made by the students themselves to use come exam time.

Learning & Class Sizes

Our class sizes are kept small to maximise the time we can spend with each pupil by taking a team teaching approach, this will enable us to teach the material while at the same time helping individuals.

We put a very strong emphasis on understanding the concepts being taught and actually teaching students how to solve problems themselves. Maths is different from other leaving cert subjects in that to succeed in the state exam rote learning doesn't work, we teach students to use the skills they learn with us and how to apply them to problems.
