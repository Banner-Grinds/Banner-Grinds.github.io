Learning & Class Sizes

Our class sizes are kept small to maximise the time we can spend with each pupil by taking a team teaching approach, this will enable us to teach the material while at the same time helping individuals

We put a very strong emphasis on understanding the concepts being taught and actually teaching students how to solve problems themselves. Maths is different from other leaving cert subjects in that to succeed in the state exam rote learning doesn't work, we teach students to use the skills they learn with us and how to apply them to problems
